## Featured Projects

**IAG – Enterprise Commercial Enablement**
Digitised commercial insurance operations across 5 brands and 20+ products. Led 20 engineers; drove full microservices architecture with Spring Boot, Kubernetes, Docker, APIGEE, Akamai, and Bamboo CI/CD.

**NAB – Kafka-Powered Microservices Suite**
Designed and delivered MS MQ Kafka Adapter (handling 22M+ daily transactions), Balance Notification (real-time Kafka + Redis), and account management APIs — all tuned with JProfiler for high-throughput production loads.

**NAB – Enterprise Data Archival Solution**
Architected a cloud-native archival platform (Airflow, AWS, Kafka, ELK) for structured and unstructured data with lifecycle policy, versioning, and metadata-indexed fast retrieval.

**Australia Post & SAI Global – eCommerce Platforms**
Led full-stack delivery on high-traffic Intershop 7.x eCommerce platforms, including microservices decomposition, AWS serverless migrations (Lambda, ECS/Fargate), PayPal/SecurePay integrations, and OKTA SSO.
